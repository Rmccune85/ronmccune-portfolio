Secure Password Manager

Download Now

A simple yet powerful tool to keep your passwords safe. This Secure Password Manager encrypts and stores your credentials locally using AES encryption and an SQLite database. No cloud storage—just **secure, offline protection** for your passwords.

Key Features:

    🔐 Strong Encryption – Protects your passwords with AES encryption.

    🔑 Master Password Security – Only you can access your saved credentials.

    📂 Local Storage – Everything is saved on your device, not the cloud.

    🎲 Built-in Password Generator – Create secure, random passwords with ease.

    🖥 Simple & Lightweight – Runs in the command line with no extra setup.
